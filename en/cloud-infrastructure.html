Cloud Infrastructure

The Cloud Infrastructure pages provide holistic information on your public cloud deployments that are governed by Netskope platform. The pages provide an aggregated overview of your inventory, compliance posture, and DLP incidents across your deployments in AWS, Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud Platform.

You can drill down into you cloud inventory in a specific region, VPC, or by tags on the Inventory dashboard.

Finally, you can look into the compliance scan results against the framework you want to measure against such as CIS, PCI, or your own framework.

To view the Cloud Infrastructure page, click Cloud Infrastructure in the Netskope UI, and then click one of the links in the left panel.
