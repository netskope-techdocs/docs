Incidents

The Incidents pages provide information about DLP incidents, anomalies, compromised credentials, and behavior analytics, plus files that have been quarantined or placed in legal hold. If you have a threat protection license, malware and malicious sites pages are shown here as well.

To view an Incidents page, click Incidents in the Netskope UI, and then click one of the links in the left panel.

Tip

Click the Refresh icon to update an Incidents page with the most current information.
