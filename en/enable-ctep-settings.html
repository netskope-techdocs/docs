Enable CTEP Settings

To enable Netskope CTEP, go to Settings > Threat Protection > CTEP Settings.

Change the status to Enabled. CTEP is applied to the type of traffic specified in the policy, not the traffic type specified here.

Now the CTEP profile selected in your Real-time Protection policies will be used by Netskope CTEP.
