Add an ADFS IdP Account in Netskope SAML – Forward Proxy

Before you begin, get your ADFS IdP URL, Entity ID, and Certificate. These are required to complete this procedure.

  1. Log in to the Netskope UI.

  2. Go to Settings > Active Platform > Forward Proxy > SAML and click Add Account.

  3. Enter a name.

    Enter the IdP URL from the ADFS: https://<adfs fqdn>/adfs/ls/..

    Enter the IDP Entity ID from the ADFS: http://<adfs fqdn>/adfs/services/trust.

    Copy the IDP Token-signing certificate into the IDP Certificate section as shown.

    Click Save.

  4. Click OK

The account is added.
