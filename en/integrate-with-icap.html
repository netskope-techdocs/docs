Integrate with ICAP

Netskope offers the capability to leverage your existing on-premises DLP investment in conjunction with the Netskope cloud solution. Netskope performs a first pass of DLP scanning in the cloud and sends the documents to the on-premise solution for the final result.

  1. Configure Secure Forwarder.

  2. Configure the Websense DSS Protector.

    1. Go to Data Security > Settings > System Modules.

    2. Make sure ICAP is enabled.

    3. Configure Protector for HTTP/HTTPS.

    4. Go to Main > Status > Traffic Log to check for traffic.

    5. View incident data.
