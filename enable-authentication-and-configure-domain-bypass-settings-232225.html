Enable Authentication and Configure Domain Bypass Settings

  1. In the Netskope tenant, go to Forward Proxy > Authentication > Enable Authentication.

  2. Select Enabled, select the account, and click Save.

  3. Click OK.

  4. Authentication is enabled.

    Go to Domain Bypass and click Edit.

  5. Enter required comma-separated URLs.

    <adfs-fqdn>,nsauth-<tenant name>.goskope.com

    Click Save.

    Important

    These URLs need to be bypassed for authentication; otherwise, the services that perform the domain bypass itself will get blocked.

  6. Click OK.

  7. Review your settings.

  8. Log off the Netskope tenant.
